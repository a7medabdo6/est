About EST

The Egyptian Scholastic Test (EST) is developed for the purpose of allowing students, in the American education system, to qualify for admissions to Egyptian higher education institutions.

It is created by experts across 5 continents and validated by the EST Board in the USA. It is designed specifically to measure the skills and knowledge acquired by students in the American education system in Egypt against common core standards.

The EST is a computer-based test delivered in collaboration with Pearson.
